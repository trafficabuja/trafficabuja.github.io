How do I place an order?
You can transact online via the home page of our website, or social media page on Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok. You can also call/WhatsApp us at +2347047744310. For over-the-counter transactions, visit our office at Michael Jackson Street, Tai Solarin Ave, Gwarinpa, FCT.
How do I get paid trading online?
We offer direct cash delivery to your specified location, or you may arrange for a third-party logistics provider of your choice to collect the cash on your behalf.
What currencies do you trade?
We trade in crisp Naira notes, along with USD, EUR, GBP, and CAD.
How long does the exchange process take?
Cash exchange is instant, though transfer times depend on the user's bank network and when the funds are received.
Can I trade currencies online for pick-up or delivery?
You can trade online while you come to pick up the cash at our office or we can deliver cash to your designated location.
Who sends currency first?
For online transactions, you have to make the payment first and we will transfer the equivalent in the preferred currency.
Can I cancel an order?
You can cancel an order as long as money hasn't exchanged hands. Once the money exchange has been made, the transaction process has started and cannot be canceled.
Do you charge?
We do not charge commissions or fees. Customers pay only the prevailing buy or sell rate for the currency.
What is the maximum amount of money I can exchange?
There is no limit to the amount you can exchange with us.
